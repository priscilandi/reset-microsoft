How to Reset a Microsoft Password

Objective

To guide you through resetting your Microsoft account password and understanding its impact on your domain password.

Prerequisites

  • Access to a web browser (e.g., Chrome).
  • Knowledge of your Microsoft account email address.
  • Ability to receive verification codes (e.g., via email or phone).

Important Note:

Be aware that if you change your Microsoft Password, your domain password will be automatically changed as well.

  1. On your Chrome page, go to office.com.
  2. Sign out.
  3. Sign in again, but before typing your password, click on Forgot my Password.
  4. Follow the password rules shown on screen.
  5. Sign In Again. After resetting, you may need to restart or sign out of your device and log in with your new password.

Process Complete

Your Microsoft password has been successfully reset.
